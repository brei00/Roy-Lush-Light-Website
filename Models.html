Skin-Centric and Long-Wear Innovations

As temperatures rise, long-wear and breathable makeup are essential. Sali Hughes highlights products like Milk’s Hydro Grip Gel Tint and Maybelline’s Super Stay 24h Skin Tint for their durability and natural finish.

Nostalgic Revival

There’s a strong nostalgic revival of ’90s and early 2000s fashion, with low-rise jeans, cargo pants, cropped cardigans, and rhinestone details making a comeback.

Snore Core

A minimalist style is trending, focusing on relaxed, neutral-toned pieces like loose khaki jeans and simple tops paired with understated accessories.

K-Beauty's Influence "Butter Skin"

Korean beauty trends are shaping summer makeup with a focus on natural, multi-functional, and expressive looks. The "butter skin" trend emphasizes a smooth, satiny complexion, moving away from glossy finishes.

Innovative Product Formats

Waterless and concentrated skincare products are emerging as sustainable alternatives. These formulations, including solid cleansers and powdered masks, reduce water usage and packaging waste while delivering potent active ingredients.

Sculptural and Statement Pieces

Bold, sculptural jewelry is making waves in 2025. Think oversized earrings, chunky rings, and dramatic cuffs that serve as focal points of an outfit. These pieces are perfect for those looking to make a strong style statement.
